# About Parvis

Parvis is a betting game tracker designed to record and analyze games played among friends and family.

## How to Play

Players make bets each round. A successful bet scores 10 points plus the bet amount. A failed bet scores 0 points.

The player with the highest total score after all rounds wins!

## Features

- **Family Tree Player Selection**: Visualize relationships and select players easily
- **Game Matrix**: Enter bets and results in a spreadsheet-like interface
- **Priority System**: The diagonal indicates who gets the tiebreaker each round
- **Statistics**: Track win rates, betting patterns, and performance over time
- **Retro Terminal Aesthetic**: Classic green phosphor CRT styling
